## **✅ HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES:**

### **1. UPLOAD TO YOUR SYSTEM:**
- ✅ Add to Learning Portal as downloadable PDFs
- ✅ Create fillable forms if possible
- ✅ Organize by template type

### **2. TRAIN YOUR ASSESSOR:**
- ✅ Show them how to complete each form
- ✅ Explain quality standards
- ✅ Practice with examples

### **3. PROVIDE TO LEARNER:**
- ✅ Include in welcome pack
- ✅ Explain each template's purpose
- ✅ Show examples of completed forms

### **4. MAINTAIN RECORDS:**
- ✅ Keep all completed forms
- ✅ Organize by learner and unit
- ✅ Ready for IQA and EQA audits
