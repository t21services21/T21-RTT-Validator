## ASSESSMENT METHODOLOGY

Our CDA materials are structured assessment tools, NOT information sheets. Each unit includes:

### 1. Learning Content (Study Materials)
- Comprehensive theory and knowledge
- Real-world examples and case studies
- Best practice guidance
- Regulatory framework
- Activities and exercises

### 2. Assessment Activities (Evidence Generation)
Each unit uses multiple assessment methods for triangulation:

| Assessment Method | Purpose | Evidence Generated |
|-------------------|---------|-------------------|
| **Professional Discussion** | Assess knowledge and understanding | Discussion record, assessor notes, audio/video |
| **Observation** | Assess practical competence | Observation record with specific examples |
| **Witness Statement** | Confirm workplace competence | Statement from manager/colleague |
| **Reflective Account** | Assess reflection and learning | Written reflection (500-1000 words) |
| **Product Evidence** | Demonstrate work outputs | Care plans, risk assessments, documents |
| **Case Study Analysis** | Assess problem-solving | Written scenario responses |
| **Practical Demonstration** | Assess practical skills | Demonstration checklist, photos/video |
| **Written Assignment** | Assess in-depth knowledge | Research and written work |

### 3. Assessment Criteria Mapping
- Each activity mapped to specific learning outcomes
- Clear demonstration of how criteria will be met
- Multiple evidence types for robust assessment
- Quality assurance through IQA sampling
